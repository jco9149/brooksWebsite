Here are a few things you can expect during our Sunday service.

What would a baptist church be without warms greetings and great fellowship? Expect to be welcomed by a member of our church family. Our church strives to embrace a welcoming atmosphere. Worship is warm, loving, and inviting as we begin services in singing worship hymns, as well as special music performances by several of our members. The word of God is proclaimed in every message as we strive to preach the Gospel (the GOOD NEWS) of Jesus Christ our Lord. 1 Cor. 2:1-5 (KJV) We preach from the true KJV bible and encourage guests to bring their bibles (KJV) with them and follow along during services. Invitations are given following each service, as we believe that the Spirit of God (The GREAT Convictor) can move upon the hearts of men and lead sinners unto the knowledge of Salvation through Jesus Christ our Lord. Eph. 2:8-9 KJV
