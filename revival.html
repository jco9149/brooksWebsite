Revival!!!

Coming July 26th, 2020

Could there be ANYTHING as needed today like REVIVAL?!

The nation is facing trials, with the constant life-altering changes concerning our day to day life as Americans; could there be anything more needed than a REVIVAL to provide us with great peace and comfort? Brooks Baptist is convinced, more than EVER BEFORE, that the ONLY HOPE in this negative world is a spritual revival in our Lord and saviour, Jesus Christ. People are more distant, divided, and confused than ever before. This revival is a way to gather those in our local community to seek peace, answers, and hope. There are more questions than answers, and the looming question on the heart of many is "What hope do we have to see us through such troubled times? "

There is a GREAT HOPE in the name of Jesus Christ. As a matter of fact, the bible tells us that in Jesus Christ we have a BLESSED HOPE(Titus 2:13). No matter how challenging the last few months have been, this church is here to tell you of the Gospel (THE GOOD NEWS) of Christ. John 3:16 tells us that, "God so loved the world (YOU), that he gave his only begotten Son, that whosoever beleiveth on him should not perish, but have everlasting life." God loves you and wants to reveal himself to you!

Come and and get revived with us in the sprirt of God, for there is no greater time than NOW to hear what God has to say to his people. Brooks is praying for the lost souls. May God continue to bless through these troubled times.

You ARE Invited!!!

Wilt thou not revive us again: that thy people may rejoice in thee?
Psalm 85:6 (KJV) ..
